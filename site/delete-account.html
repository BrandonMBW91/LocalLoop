Delete your Local Loop account

You can delete your Local Loop account and everything tied to it at any time. Here are two ways to do it.

In the app (fastest)

  1. Open the Local Loop app.
  2. Go to Settings.
  3. Tap Delete Account.
  4. Confirm. Your account and everything you posted is removed right away.

By email

If you can no longer open the app, email localloop@localloop.io from the email address on your account and ask us to delete it. We will remove your account and data within 30 days and reply to confirm.

What gets deleted

  • Your account and email address.
  • Any events, garage sales, or food trucks you posted, including photos.
  • Your saved listings, interests, and followed venues.
  • Usage and analytics data tied to your device.

Anonymized, aggregated statistics that cannot identify you may be kept.
