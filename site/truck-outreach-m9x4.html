Food truck outreach checklist

Check a truck off once you've grabbed its public email from Facebook (About section) or sent it a Messenger pitch. Progress saves on this device. Only take emails that are publicly listed.

Calendar-first message to send (FB DM or email)

Hi! I'm Michael, I run Local Loop, a free Ohio app people open to find food trucks near them. Here's the easy part for you: if you keep a Google Calendar of your stops, send me the link once and your whole schedule shows up in the app automatically. You never post anything. It's free, and it puts your truck in front of locals who are already looking for somewhere to eat. Want in? Just reply with your calendar link (or "tell me more").
