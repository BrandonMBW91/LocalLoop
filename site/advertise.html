How the price works

You always pay your own town's current rate. The rate is set by how many people use Local Loop in that town each month, so it starts low and only steps up as more of your neighbors join. Every town is at the Founding rate today. Sign up now and your rate is locked in for a full year, even as the town grows.

Tier Town size (people using the app) Town Sponsor Featured 30 days
FoundingEvery town today Starting out $19/mo $25

Established 250 or more $49/mo $49
Premier 1,000 or more $79/mo $79

The 7-day Featured option follows the same tiers: $9, $12, $19, then $29. The All of NW Ohio plan is a single flat $79/mo across every town.

How it works

  1. Pick a plan above and check out securely with Stripe (card or Apple/Google Pay). Prefer to talk first? Email us.
  2. Tell us about your ad: your business name, headline, link, and town.
  3. You go live in the app, with simple monthly billing.
  4. Ask us for your numbers any time: views and taps, so you see the reach you're getting.

Questions, or want to feature an event for a single weekend? Email localloop@localloop.io and we'll set you up.
